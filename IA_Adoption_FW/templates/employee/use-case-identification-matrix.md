> **Purpose**: Identify high-value AI use cases for personal productivity

## How to Use This Template

1. **Brainstorm**: List 5-10 tasks you do regularly
2. **Score**: Rate each task using criteria below (1-5 scale)

4. **Select**: Pick top 3 tasks with highest priority scores

---

## Scoring Criteria

### Frequency (How often?)
- **5**: Multiple times per day
- **4**: Daily
- **3**: 2-3 times per week
- **2**: Weekly
- **1**: Monthly or less

### Time per Task (How long?)
- **5**: >2 hours
- **4**: 1-2 hours
- **3**: 30min-1h
- **2**: 15-30min
- **1**: <15min

### AI Suitability (Can AI help?)
- **5**: Perfect (writing, code generation, research)
- **4**: High (analysis, brainstorming, templates)
- **3**: Medium (editing, formatting, summaries)
- **2**: Low (requires human judgment)
- **1**: Not suitable (sensitive decisions, creative strategy)
